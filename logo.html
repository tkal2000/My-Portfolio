CareerCove – Online Job Portal Logo
Designed using Adobe Illustrator, this logo represents CareerCove, an online job portal. The magnifying glass element symbolizes job search and career focus, while the layered curves and vibrant green palette convey growth, opportunity, and a modern digital presence.

Kepz – Food Ordering System Logo
Crafted in Adobe Illustrator, the Kepz logo captures the essence of smart food ordering. The chef hat, dish cover, fork, and spoon icons are creatively combined into a circular form, highlighting the theme “Keep Eat Perfect Easy.” The black-and-white aesthetic emphasizes elegance and simplicity.
